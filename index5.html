Tournament Rules

No Hacking or Cheating

Any form of hacking, cheating, or using unauthorized third-party software will result in immediate disqualification and permanent ban from future tournaments.

No Teaming

Players must not team up with opponents from other teams. Any suspicious behavior will be reviewed and may lead to penalties.

Fair Play

All players must maintain sportsmanship and respect towards opponents and organizers. Toxic behavior will not be tolerated.

Punctuality

All teams must be present at least 15 minutes before the scheduled match time. Late arrivals may face penalties or disqualification.

Connection Issues

Players are responsible for their own internet connection. No rematches will be provided due to individual connection problems.
